Soft Nano Electronics Lab

Our laboratory focuses on the development of national core technologies in the fields of nanotechnology and materials engineering. In particular, we conduct research on Stationary Plasma Thrusters, Flexible Electronics, ZnO-based Quantum Dots, Ultra high Barrier and Thin Film Encapsulation, with the aim of opening up new application areas and contributing to the future development of industry and technology. We expect that our research will play a significant role in shaping the future of these fields.
